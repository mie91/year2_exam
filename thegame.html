How to play

Click on the Dice on the left side to roll it, and to advance on the board. But watch out for traps! They can ruin your process on the board, and make you go backwards. To finish the game, you'll have to reach the "Goal!" tile. You must get the exact number of remaining tiles on your dice to reach the Goal. When the Goal is reached, you have WON the game!
